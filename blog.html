The Fare Well Files

The Fare Well Files, my Substack blog, began as a place to write about how I left my career as a lawyer to become a full-time father and creative. It quickly became a home for any idea that starts trying to consume chunks of my brain.

Now, my stated intention is to make this the least categorizable publication on Substack. So far, I've written about fatherhood, ambition, law, writing, AI, and religion, to name a few. Feel free to browse them by publication date, or filter them by topic.
