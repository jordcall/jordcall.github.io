Bylines

For a while, I put effort into doing freelance writing for established publications. This was a lot of fun, but it meant that my creative time became ninety-nine parts pitching to editors for every one part writing, which didn't feel aligned with what I wanted to be doing. I haven't pitched anything in a long time, and I might return to it someday, but for now I'm happy writing my blog, where I spend zero parts pitching for every hundred parts writing thanks to my blog.
