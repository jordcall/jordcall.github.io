Djenius Guitar Practice Tracker

Djenius is an early-release Android app I built for tracking and logging guitar practice, with a gypsy-jazz-ish bias in how I think about structure, repertoire, and time.

It’s not on the Play Store yet. I’m sharing it here for curious people who want to try it, kick the tires, and send feedback.

Install

  1. Download the APK.
  2. On Android, allow installs from unknown sources for your browser/files app if prompted.
  3. Tap the APK to install.
  4. If Android blocks it, it’s because the app isn’t from the Play Store; you can choose to proceed.
