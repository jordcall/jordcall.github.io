Music

I spent my youth in the classical music world, competing in piano competitions and composing orchestral pieces. Then I branched out into the world of pop and rock and formed a few bands, building chops on drums, guitar, and bass. Then I found a home in gypsy jazz guitar. My dream of recording music led me to a Masters in Music Production program at Berklee (I'm halfway through).

Now, I have various projects across various genres, on both sides of the glass, as it were. Sometimes I produce or mix, sometimes I write songs, sometimes I perform on various instruments. Check out some of my favorite projects below.
