Writing

Writing may be what I do best. I built my chops as an English undergrad and then got about a hundred times better in the crucible of my legal career. This involved fun writing exercises like having my work shredded by appellate judges and co-authoring briefs for the Supreme Court in a state of abject terror.

I’ve written for various publications online, which you can explore under Bylines. My latest project is a Substack publication called The Fare Well Files, which I’m pouring my soul into. If you care to put your ear up to my heart and hear what it’s pumping right now, just subscribe to that.
